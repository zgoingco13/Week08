About Me!

My Experience

I love playing a lot of sports, and used to play soccer competitively when I was younger. However, I am limited to many sports due to knee injuries.

Counterintutive, but my gummy knees actually feel fine while rock climbing. I have been climbing for almost one year! I mostly climb indoors because it is more accessible, but climbing outdoors is a lot more fun because it is more challenging.

The LCC!

What is LCC?

Love, care, and cupcakes.
Jk, but almost the same! LCC is the Ladies Climbing Coalition that brings together female and non-binary climbers together.

I am part of the LCC LA chapter where we meetup the second Thursday of every month and even climb outdoors. Click here for info about LCC!
