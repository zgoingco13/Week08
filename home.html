Let's Go Rockclimbing

What is rock climbing?

Rock climbing is incredibly fun sport that incorporates a full body work. You can always climb indoors or have some
fun in the sun and climb outdoors!

There are many ways to climb, but here are the three main ways:

Type of Rock Climb Example Description
Bouldering (Free Solo)

Climbing without the aid of any ropes. Generally walls are shorter.
Top Rope

Climbing with a rope where a belayer feeds the rope through a pulley system. (Safest way to climb imo)

Lead Climbing

Combo of bouldering and top rope. Belayer is feeding the rope which climber attaches to every clip during climb. Pulley system only after rope is clipped in.
